## Key Decisions / Insights
*   **Taxonomy is limited:** Classifying existing datasets is inherently subjective due to "task impurity."
*   **Subtraction is superior:** "Paired Tasks" (Baseline vs. Manipulation) provide objective mathematical proof of cognitive bottlenecks without needing labels.
*   **Dorsal Blindness confirmed:** The review supports the hypothesis that current models fail specifically at "Where" (Spatial/Dorsal) and "Inhibition" (Executive), while succeeding at "What" (Ventral).
